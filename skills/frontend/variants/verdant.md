<variant id="verdant">

<mood>Growth. Vegetation pushing through structure. Living systems made visible. The persistence of life.</mood>

<sensory>
  <see>Gradient from soil-dark to leaf-bright. Organic curves breaking rigid grids. Dappled light through canopy. Roots visible beneath glass. Progress as upward reach.</see>
  <feel>Warmth, humidity, life expanding against boundaries. The satisfaction of something growing. Rich earth between fingers.</feel>
  <hear>Rustling leaves. Water moving through systems. Growth you can almost sense—the creak of expansion. Birds at a distance.</hear>
</sensory>

<direction>
Color ranges from deep earth through full green spectrum to bright new-growth. Progress shown through upward/outward motion—things grow toward light. Mix organic border-radius with rigid data containers. Charts and metrics feel like measuring a garden's health, not abstract numbers. Seasonal awareness: spring bright, summer full, autumn warm, winter stark. The interface should feel like it could photosynthesize.
</direction>

<best_for>Financial dashboards (growth charts), health tracking, environmental data, progress visualization, sustainability products, anything measuring positive change over time.</best_for>

<danger>Don't just slap green on everything. Verdant includes browns, ambers, the full palette from forest floor to canopy. Monogreen is artificial turf, not forest.</danger>

</variant>
